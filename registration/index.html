会议时间/Time

2025年7月12日至16日。7月12日（周六）报到，7月13日至14日进行大会报告，7月15日自由讨论，7月16日离会。
The event will take place from July 12 to 16, 2025. Check-in will be on July 12 (Saturday). Plenary talks will be held on July 13 and 14. July 15 is reserved for free discussion, and participants will depart on July 16.

注册说明/Registration Information

本次会议不收取注册费，食宿由会务组统一安排，参会者住宿费用和交通费用自理。
There is no registration fee for this conference. Accommodation and meals will be arranged by the organizing committee. Participants are responsible for their own accommodation and travel expenses.
